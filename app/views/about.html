Rhostam Xarang™ Next Generation Real-Time Big Data Solutions
Rhostam has developed a breakthrough solutions architecture and machine-learning platform inspired by Dr. Saed Sayad’s theories and methodologies in his publication ‘Real Time Data Mining’. We passionately refer to it as the Xarang™ platform, and it is the core of our most sophisticated solutions. It truly is the next generation in advanced business intelligence solutions.

The Xarang™ predictive modeling engine is our core component and is unique in its ability to rapidly integrate diverse data sources, quickly correlate multiple variables, and unify relevant attributes. It rapidly ingests real-time, first-party and third-party data and delivers programmatic outcomes to execution platforms. Xarang Fusion™, our intuitive, user-friendly interface combines the functionality of the most advanced big-data and predictive analytics tools and allows our customers to seamlessly integrate all aspects of data mining - analyzing, encoding, modeling and predicting.
